Selected Projects

Below are some of the Finance and Data Projects I've created while attending Northeastern

OVX & RBOB — Event-Driven Oil & Gas Analysis

Analyzed how sanctions, OPEC decisions, wars, and outbreaks relate to OVX volatility and RBOB gasoline futures; built visuals and correlation matrices to characterize short-, mid-, and long-horizon effects.

  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Time Series

Pairs Trading — Cointegration & Regime Detection

Built a market-neutral trading strategy using Engle–Granger tests, Z-Score spread signals, and Gaussian HMM regimes; evaluated Sharpe, drawdown, and live signal quality on ETF pairs.

  • Python
  • Statsmodels
  • Cointegration
  • HMM
  • Backtesting

STOCKSIM — Monte Carlo Stock Forecasting

Simulated 1,000 Geometric Brownian Motion paths per equity ticker from yfinance; estimated μ/σ, target-hit probabilities, and 95% price intervals; visualized paths and scenario bounds for investor decision support.

  • Python
  • NumPy
  • Matplotlib
  • GBM
  • Monte Carlo
